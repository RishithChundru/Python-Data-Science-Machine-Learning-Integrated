->What is Power BI?
	* It is a business analytics tool developed  by Microsoft that enables users to 	  visualize and analyze data with greater speed, efficiency and understanding.
	* It offers interactive visualizations, business intelligence capabilities, and an 	  easy-to-use interface for creating reports and dashboards.

->Components of Power BI:
	* Power BI Desktop
	* Power BI Service(Online Software as a Service(SaaS))
	* Power BI Mobile Apps
	* Power BI Report Server.

->Key Features of Power BI:
	* Data Connectivity
	* Data Transformation
	* Visualizations
	* Dashboards and Reports
	* DAX(Data Analysis Expressions).

->Benefits:
	* Ease to use
	* Real-time insights
	* Collaboration and sharing
	* Cost-effective
	* Integration with other tools.

->Who can benefit from power BI?
	* Business analyst, data analysts and data scientists.
	* Decision-makers and executives.
	* IT professionals.
