->Major Roles in Data domain:

	1)Data Analyst:
	  Skills: SQL, Excel, Data visualization, basic statistical Knowledge.
	* They analyse the trends. 
	* They visualize the data.

	2)Data Scientist:
	  Skills: Programming(Python/R),machine learning, advanced statistics, data 			  wrangling.
	* They develop algorithms.
	* They work on predictive models.
	* They perform advanced data analyses.
	* They pre-process the data.
	
	3)Machine Learning Engineer:
	  Skills: Deep Learning, algorithm optimization, programming, software engineering.
	* They develop ML models.
	* They optimize ML algorithms.
	* They also work on ML integrated systems.

	4)Data Engineer:
	  Skills: Database Management, ETL processes, big data technologies, data pipelines.
	* They work on database.
	* They work on ETL.
	* They work on data pipelines.

	5)Business Intelligence Analyst
	  Skills: Data analysis, BI tools(Tableau, Power BI), business knowledge.
	* They are responsible for analysing business data.
	* They generate reports.
	* They are used for decision making.
